About Aurreum

We are committed to providing trusted data protection and data management products and solutions to help customers achieve digital transformation.

We provide a single platform for Cloud, Virtual, Physical, SaaS and Kubernetes environments.

Our Vision

We are one world. We seek to create a safe world without artificial barriers or imposed limitations. As humans, we have come from cave paintings to digital arts previously unimaginable. Although what requires protection in our lives has changed, the significance of protecting what is precious has not. Global customers trust Aurreum to safeguard their digital treasures from a wide range of threats, including (not limited to) hardware failures, natural disasters, human mistakes, and cyber-attacks. Aurreum provides innovative, comprehensive, and affordable solutions for data protection through on-premise deployment or cloud services. United, we protect your business and your future. We are continually innovating to redefine the data protection golden standard.

Our Customers

Customer satisfaction is our top priority. All customers, large or small, are equal to Aurreum. We are happy only when all our customers are satisfied. We build our partner and channel programs with the sole intention of satisfying our customers. We empower our partners with technical innovations in data protection. United, we grow our businesses together.

Our Culture

We are a family. We thrive by our five core values: Care, Happiness, Integrity, Novelty, and Adaptability. We care for each other in the family, share happiness in our journey to greatness, treat customers with integrity, invent novel products to protect business data, and continuously adapt to the ever-changing needs of our customers. United, we build our future and help our customers through teamwork and innovation.

Our Ecosystem

We firmly believe that our solutions should be open and integrated with an ecosystem of partners to accommodate our customer’s IT environments. Thus, our solutions are compatible with mainstream operating systems, databases, file systems, storage systems, and cloud platforms, providing flexible data protection options for any heterogeneous IT environment. With an innovative engineering team, we can rapidly refine our software to adapt to the continuous evolution of IT environments.

Aurreum works with industry-leading hardware and software partners and system integrators.Together with our technology alliance partners, we provide trustworthy data protection solutions to global customers.

Our Leadership

Our leadership has all worked in well-known international companies on data protection in the past. We have served customers in all industries and have an average of more than 15 years of work experience in data protection. With the inspiration and guidance of our leadership team, we are continually exploring new technologies to address the data protection needs of evolving IT environments.
