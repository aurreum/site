Aurreum released the industry's most reliable cloud data management platform.

Fri, Apr 21, 2023 Admin

Aurreum, a rising star in the field of multi-cloud data management, released its self-developed Cloud Data Protection Services today. This service will help enterprises effectively protect data in the cloud, avoid data loss, defend against Ransomware attacks, and achieve simple and unified cloud data management.

The rise of multi-cloud creates a transformative infrastructure. However, customers own the security, protection, compliance, and availability of their data within the cloud as part of a shared responsibility model with the cloud service provider (CSP). Tools offered by CSPs deliver basic functionality, but mission-critical applications require enterprise-grade capabilities, optimized cost management and cross-cloud data mobility and visibility.

Aurreum CEO Jacob said: “Although we are a newly established data management company, our product and R&D teams have 15 years of experience in enterprise-level data management innovation in the field to meet the unique needs of the cloud. The launch of Cloud Data Protection Services empower customers to own and control their data no matter where it resides—on-prem or in the cloud—while reducing the operational complexity and costs associated with managing multi-cloud environments.”

Control Data Anywhere

Aurreum Cloud Data Protection Services provides a web-based management control platform, allows customers to manage their entire data protection estate, on-prem and in the cloud, from a single pane of glass. Rather than oversee multiple solutions for disparate on-prem and cloud workloads, customers can leverage a single management console for data in any location across multiple domains.

Not only that, users can also get timely audit logs and alarm information via email or SMS, and quickly locate the root cause of abnormal operations.

Main advantage of Cloud Data Protection Services

Hybrid multi-cloud support: Aurreum provides a simple wizard interface to help users set up accounts in their private clouds or link their accounts to public clouds, including AWS, Azure, Google Cloud, and IBM cloud.The cloud data protection service can backup data into multiple storage targets based on users' preferences, providing a more reliable data protection service.

Cross-cloud data replication and disaster recovery: Replicating users' backup data from their private clouds or their current public cloud storage into object storage in another public cloud allows users to promptly bring the business IT services up by instant disaster recovery in case of data center failures.

Ransomware protection: While routine backup is an excellent practice against ransomware attacks, Aurreum can strengthen the protection by coordinating with the cloud storage providers to store the backup data into immutable object storage in a Write-Once-Read-Many (WORM), non-erasable and non-rewritable manner.

In case of a Ransomware attack, bare metal recovery (BMR) and instant recovery techniques can help quickly bring the business IT services back to life at local servers, the private cloud, or a public cloud.

Even if public cloud service providers provide data protection promises, it is always the enterprise’s own responsibility to protect its own data security. Aurreum can make up for the limitations of public cloud service providers in terms of data protection and provide users with more flexible data protection strategies.

What’s more worth mentioning is that Aurreum uses industry-standard 256-bit AES encryption to encrypt users' data for backup and transfer to the cloud and user data privacy will be guaranteed to the greatest extent.

The Aurreum cloud data protection service also provides a free usage period of up to 30 days. Users only need to download the installation package on the official website, and in a few simple steps, we can easily experience the convenient cloud service.
