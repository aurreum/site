Aurreum released the industry's most reliable cloud data management platform

Fri, Apr 21, 2023

The breakout innovator Aurreum International has just released its Cloud Data Protection Service. Built from the ground up by the engineers of Aurreum, the Cloud Data Protection Service aims to bring effective multi-cloud data management tools to IT professionals of small businesses and enterprises. This service will help enterprises effectively protect data in the cloud, avoid data loss, defend against ransomware attacks, and achieve simple and unified cloud data management.

The rise of multi-cloud creates a transformative infrastructure. However, customers own the security, protection, compliance, and availability of their data within the cloud as part of a shared responsibility model with the cloud service provider (CSP). Tools offered by CSPs deliver basic functionality, but mission-critical applications require enterprise-grade capabilities, optimized cost management and cross-cloud data mobility and visibility.

Aurreum CEO, Jacob, said: “Although we are a newly established data management company, our product and R&D teams have a collective 20 years’ experience in enterprise-level data management innovation in the field to meet the unique needs of the cloud. The launch of Cloud Data Protection Services empowers customers to own and control their data no matter where it resides – on-prem or in the cloud – while reducing the operational complexity and costs associated with managing multi-cloud environments.”

Control Data Anywhere and Anytime

Aurreum Cloud Data Protection Services provides a fine-tuned web-based management console, that allows customers to manage all of their data assets, on-prem and in the cloud, from a single pane of glass. Rather than oversee multiple solutions for incongruent on-prem and cloud workloads, customers can leverage a single management console for the data in any location across multiple domains.

Not only that, users can also get timely audit logs and alarm information via email or SMS, and quickly locate the root cause of abnormal operations.

Main Advantage of Cloud Data Protection Services

Hybrid Multi-cloud Support: Aurreum provides a simple operation and automation interface to help users set up accounts in their private cloud or link their accounts to public clouds, including AWS, Azure, Google Cloud, and IBM cloud. The cloud data protection services can backup into multiple storage targets based on users' preferences, providing a more reliable data protection service.

Multi-tenant Support: For a business with multiple departments, CDPS provides multi-tenant support to allow several subaccounts under the main account to operate their data protection tasks independently, providing data isolation and adaptable information security within the organization.

Ransomware Protection: While routine backup is an excellent practice against ransomware attacks, Aurreum can strengthen the protection by coordinating with the cloud storage providers to store the backup data into immutable object storage in a Write-Once-Read-Many (WORM), non-erasable and non-rewritable manner.

Bare metal recovery (BMR) and instant recovery techniques protect against ransomware attacks and help quickly bring the business IT services back to life on local servers, the private cloud, or a public cloud.

Even if public cloud service providers provide data protection promises, it is always the enterprise’s own responsibility to protect its own data security. Aurreum can make up for the limitations of public cloud service providers in terms of data protection and provide users with more flexible data protection strategies.

Aurreum employs industry-standard 256-bit AES encryption to protect users’ data for backup and transfer to the cloud and user data privacy in order to comply with the highest standards.

30-Day Free Trial

The Aurreum Cloud Data Protection Service also provides a free usage period of up to 30 days. Users only need to download the installation package on the official website, and in a few simple steps, you can effortlessly discover how Aurreum’s solutions can benefit your company.
