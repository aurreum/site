Aurreum Data Protection Suite

Single Platform to Protect ALL Workloads

Your ubiquitous data is your most valuable assets. Protect it !

As the most valuable resource for businesses in the interconnected era, data can help a company meaningfully engage with customers, make business decisions, add value to the organization, and better inform product and service development. Losing valuable data may cause the business to flounder and even shutdown. Aurreum Data Protection Suite (ADPS) integrates backup/restore, disaster recovery, replication, Continuous Data Protection (CDP), Copy Data Management (CDM), and Bare Metal Recovery (BMR) into a single unified platform to address the data protection needs of all businesses.

Worry-free ransomware protection

In this interconnected world, no organization is immune to sophisticated ransomware attacks. Aurreum Data Protection Suite (ADPS) can help a business recover its valuable data and continue the operation without paying the ransom. ADPS can simultaneously replicate the backup data into different storage devices at various locations, including offline storage in which the backup data will not be affected by ransomware attacks. Furthermore, with continuous log shipping and any point recovery techniques, ADPS can quickly restore files and databases to the point right before the cyber-attack to ensure business continuity.

Speedy backup and instant recovery

ADPS utilizes various acceleration techniques, including compression, deduplication, change block tracking (CBT), instant recovery, etc., to speed up the backup/restore process. These techniques ensure users can promptly recover their data and applications to minimize business downtime.

Hybrid data protection.

Business data may exist in physical servers, virtual machines, private clouds, and public clouds. Nowadays, the ever-complicated infrastructure, including the cloud, has become increasingly fragile. ADPS can flexibly backup and replicate business data into local storage devices, private clouds, and public clouds based on predefined data protection policies, providing a reliable data protection solution.

Exploiting the value of your backup data

Business decisions need data analytics, and data analytics require data. Retrieving the data from production servers for data analytics may impact the business because these servers are often overwhelmed by the massive online access. Backup data in a traditional backup system are only used for disaster recovery drills or actual disaster recoveries. With seamless integration of CDM, ADPS can provide any-point and any-time data copies for disaster recovery, data analytics, software testing, etc., maximizing the utilization of backup data.

Great compatibility

ADPS provide various ways to protect the data for servers, databases, virtual environments, cloud and big data platforms, file systems, and applications. Its compatibility list includes (not limited to):
  • Databases: Oracle, SQL Server, MySQL, MongoDB, PostgreSQL, RDS for MySQL, SAP HANA, Caché, etc.
  • Virtual Environments: VMware, Hyper-V, KVM, openStack, Xen, Docker, Kurbernets, etc.
  • File System: POSIX(FILE), NAS(NDMP), Hadoop(HDFS), OSS(S3, Azure)
  • Applications：Exchange, Sharepoint, Domino
  • Big data environment：Hadoop
