Aurreum Cloud Data Protection Service

Solve data protection problems with simpler, flexible and reliable solutions.
Concerning the data loss of your local computers due to accidents, hardware failures, or human mistakes? Afraid of ransomware attacks? Unease about your cloud data due to the rising number of data center disasters? We get your back. Aurreum provides a managed cloud data protection service for businesses of any size, anywhere, and any time. Backing up your valuable business data in hybrid clouds, Aurreum can quickly bring your IT service online through cloud-based instant recovery.

Web-based management console

A Web-based management console allows you to manage the cloud backup service from any device as long as there is an internet connection. With an interface Wizard, the setup and management are straightforward, and a few simple steps will bring your data protection service up and running. A graphical dashboard displays a real-time glance of the entire data protection service, including protected resources, running jobs, active alerts, and available storage space.

Configurable auditing and alert

A configurable auditing and alert system monitors user operations and service activities and notifies users of abnormal actions by email or SMS. With auditing logs and alert messages, not only can users be promptly informed of the service problems, but also they can quickly identify the causes of the problems.

Encrypted backup and restore

Aurreum uses industry-standard 256-bit AES encryption to encrypt users' data for backup and transfer to the cloud. The backup data in the cloud is encrypted using the encryption key (known only to the user if it is a private encryption key). Because Aurreum does not save the private encryption key anywhere, it is the user's responsibility to archive the key safely unless users opt for the default encryption key.

Multi-tenant support

For a business with multiple departments, ACDPS provides multi-tenant support to allow several subaccounts under the main account to operate their data protection tasks independently, providing data isolation and adaptable information security within the organization.

Hybrid multi-cloud support

Aurreum provides a simple wizard interface to help users set up accounts in their private clouds or link their accounts to public clouds, including AWS, Azure, Google Cloud, and IBM cloud. The cloud data protection service can backup data into multiple storage targets based on users' preferences, providing a more reliable data protection service.

Cross-cloud data replication and disaster recovery

Replicating users' backup data from their private clouds or their current public cloud storage into object storage in another public cloud allows users to promptly bring the business IT services up by instant disaster recovery in case of data center failures.

Ransomware protection

While routine backup is an excellent practice against ransomware attacks, Aurreum can strengthen the protection by coordinating with the cloud storage providers to store the backup data into immutable object storage in a Write-Once-Read-Many (WORM), non-erasable and non-rewritable manner. In case of a ransomware attack, bare metal recovery (BMR) and instant recovery techniques can help quickly bring the business IT services back to life at local servers, the private cloud, or a public cloud.
